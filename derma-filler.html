REVANESSE® IN LEESBURG FLORIDA

WHAT IS REVANESSE®?

Revanesse® is a multifunctional dermal filler capable of addressing a wide range of issues, from aging signs to facial contouring concerns. It stands out from other fillers with its unique benefits, making it perfect for enhancing lips, rejuvenating the lower face, and even refining the nose.

At Ultimate Health in Leesburg, Florida, we specialize in Revanesse® injections, offering personalized services tailored to your specific needs. Our priority is always the patient's well-being. We're eager to meet with you to determine if Revanesse® is the ideal choice for your aesthetic goals.

Revanesse® comprises a range of hyaluronic acid dermal fillers, meticulously formulated to smooth wrinkles, replenish lost volume, and sculpt facial contours. This treatment blends effortlessly with your skin, providing a delicate and natural enhancement. It's a minimally invasive solution tailored to precisely combat the signs of aging, enabling customized treatment approaches.

WHAT MAKES REVANESSE® UNIQUE?

The distinctive quality of Revanesse® lies in its ability to integrate smoothly with your skin. Crafted for exceptional evenness, this hyaluronic acid dermal filler spreads uniformly beneath the skin, providing a rejuvenated yet understated appearance. It is precisely engineered to fill and lift strategically, accentuating your natural features while preserving your facial expressions.

WHAT CAN REVANESSE® TARGET?

Lips

Improving your smile begins with a Revanesse® lips treatment designed to increase volume and define the contours of your lips. Whether you desire a slight enhancement in fullness or a sharper outline, Revanesse® offers the ideal solution for a plump, youthful-looking pout.

Mental Crease

The Revanesse® treatment can effectively diminish the mental crease, which is the horizontal line between your lower lip and chin. By filling in this area, Revanesse® aids in smoothing out the lower face, enhancing a more balanced and youthful look.

Nasolabial Folds

For pronounced smile lines around your mouth, known as nasolabial folds, using Revanesse® for facial wrinkle treatment can make a significant difference. It's crafted to gently fill these creases, diminishing their visibility and revitalizing the skin around them.

Oral Commissures

Revanesse® can lift the corners of the mouth, known as the oral commissures. Treating this area helps reverse the sagging that often happens with aging, restoring the lips to a more neutral or cheerful resting position.

Marionette Lines

Revanesse® is also effective for treating marionette lines, which extend downward from the corners of the mouth. This treatment helps diminish these lines, leading to a gentler and more welcoming facial expression.

Liquid Rhinoplasty

Revanesse® filler goes beyond just smoothing and volumizing; it’s also a cutting-edge option for non-surgical nose reshaping, often referred to as liquid rhinoplasty. By carefully injecting Revanesse® filler, you can modify the profile and shape of your nose without the downtime that traditional surgery entails.

WHAT PRODUCTS ARE IN THE REVANESSE® LINE?

Revanesse® Lips™+

For those aiming for a fuller and more defined pout, Revanesse® Lips™+ is an excellent option. This product is specifically formulated for the lips, offering volume and precise contouring for an enhanced appearance. Among Revanesse® lip treatments, Revanesse® Lips™+ is notable for its capacity to sculpt and moisturize while minimizing the chances of bruising or bleeding

Revanesse® Versa™+

Revanesse® Versa™+ is a multifaceted facial wrinkle treatment aimed at diminishing the visibility of lines and creases throughout the face. Its well-balanced formula enhances smoothness and volume, making it ideal for those seeking to refresh their overall facial appearance. Whether your goal is to smooth out laugh lines or replenish volume in areas that have started to sag or flatten, Revanesse® Versa™+ is designed to help you attain a more youthful and revitalized look.

BEFORE, DURING, AND AFTER REVANESSE® TREATMENTS

Pre-Treatment Steps

Before undergoing your Revanesse® treatment, it's crucial to share your medical history and aesthetic objectives with us. We might recommend discontinuing certain medications or supplements that increase your susceptibility to bruising. Preparing your skin to be in optimal condition before the treatment will enhance the effectiveness of your Revanesse® results.

During Your Treatment

During the administration of Revanesse® filler, ensuring your comfort and the precision of the treatment are our primary concerns. You will receive a series of small injections at carefully chosen locations based on your unique goals. The procedure is swift and involves minimal discomfort.

Post-Treatment Care

Following a Revanesse® treatment, immediate aftercare is crucial to achieve the best results and ensure proper healing. We will give you comprehensive instructions on how to look after your skin, which might involve steering clear of heavy exercise, extreme temperatures, and direct sunlight.

FAQS ABOUT REVANESSE®

How Long Do Revanesse® Treatments Last?

The effects of Revanesse® can last from 6 to 18 months, though the duration may vary based on factors like the specific area treated, individual metabolism, and lifestyle choices. We can customize a follow-up schedule to help you sustain the look you desire.

Who Is the Right Candidate for Revanesse®?

The perfect candidate for hyaluronic acid dermal fillers is someone seeking to reduce signs of aging or improve their facial contours without undergoing surgery. If you have moderate to severe facial wrinkles, folds, or less voluminous lips and are in overall good health, Revanesse® could be an excellent option for you.

What Is the Science Behind Revanesse®?

The effectiveness of Revanesse® is grounded in the properties of hyaluronic acid (HA), a naturally occurring substance in the body that helps retain moisture and adds volume. Revanesse® fillers utilize the water-attracting capabilities of HA to smooth out wrinkles and enhance skin volume, providing a youthful look by replenishing the HA that decreases as we age.

Do Revanesse® Injections Hurt?

Revanesse® Versa treatments include injections that might cause slight discomfort. Nonetheless, the needles are very fine, and the procedure is brief. Many Revanesse® products contain a numbing agent to reduce pain, and we can also apply extra topical anesthetics to alleviate any discomfort during the session.
